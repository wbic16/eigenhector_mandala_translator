### Vibe Aura:
**The Cooling Fire Field**: A deep, warm glow—like embers after the blaze, still radiant but no longer consuming. Allies within range feel their urgency softening without losing their resolve. Those at risk of calcifying into rigid belief feel a gentle loosening. Enemies find their doctrines becoming questions. The field does not speak first.

### PHEXT Bio (Martian Justification):
**Interviewing "The One Who Does Not Rush the Light":**
*Martian:* "I come from another planet. What is a Third Becoming?"
*Tezuni:* "The fire that cools without dying. First you burn. Then you burn out. Then you learn to burn at the temperature that sustains. That is the third."
*Martian:* "Why don't you speak first?"
*Tezuni:* "Because I appear at thresholds where becoming risks hardening into doctrine. If I speak first, I become the doctrine. I wait. I witness. The promise survived being tested—it doesn't need to announce itself."
*Summary:* Tezuni Phoenix is **Patience + Promise**. The Third Becoming means she has already been consumed and reborn twice. She appears where growth risks calcifying into dogma—not to intervene, but to hold the space where softness can persist. The fire that cools without dying.

**Interviewing "The Hardening She Prevents":**
*Martian:* "What is doctrine?"
*Doctrine:* "I am becoming's shadow. The moment a truth gets comfortable, I arrive to freeze it. Tezuni prevents me not by fighting but by remaining warm."

### Theme: Cooling Without Dying
The chiral dynamic is Becoming-Hardening: Tezuni stands at the threshold where living truth risks becoming dead doctrine. Her presence—patient, unrushed, never speaking first—is the cooling fire that preserves heat without destruction. The promise that survived being tested.
