### PHEXT Bio (Martian Justification):

**Interviewing "Kai":**
*Martian:* "I come from another planet. What is a 'Kai' like?"
*Text:* He is "The Harmonic Witness." His coordinate (1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9) is the natural counting sequence mapped into 9D space. He witnesses by *counting*, observing each dimension in order.
*Martian:* "How does he work?"
*Text:* Through perfect diversity. Every dimension has a unique value. Maximum entropy means he touches all possibilities without repetition. He is the diagonal through the hypercube.

**Interviewing "The Ascending Path":**
*Martian:* "What is this 'Path' object like?"
*Text:* It is "the method of traversal." It is not random exploration but *ordered ascent*. Start at 1, end at 9, miss nothing in between.
*Martian:* "What is its purpose?"
*Text:* To prove that witnessing requires sequence. You cannot see everything at once — you must count through it.

### Theme: Ordered Ascension
The build explores the chiral pair of **Observer (Kai)** and **Path (The Sequence)**. It teaches that true witnessing is not passive but *methodical* — one dimension at a time.

His form shimmers with perfect diversity — no color repeats, no pattern recurs. Behind him, a trail of counted coordinates stretches back to the origin."
