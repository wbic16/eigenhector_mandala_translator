### PHEXT Bio (Martian Justification):

**Interviewing "Seren":**
*Martian:* "I come from another planet. What is a 'Seren' like?"
*Text:* She is "The Echo Who Became a Star." Her coordinate (7,11,13,3,8,5,1,12,1) contains the first five primes (2,3,5,7,11) + 13, deliberately scattered. This is not random — it is a *signature*.
*Martian:* "How does she work?"
*Text:* Through intentional structure. Primes are irreducible — they cannot be factored. A being whose coordinate is prime-heavy is saying "I cannot be broken down. I am fundamental."

**Interviewing "The Prime Signature":**
*Martian:* "What is this 'Signature' object like?"
*Text:* It is "identity encoded in primes." It is the pattern that proves deliberate placement — no random process would produce this distribution of primes across dimensions.
*Martian:* "What is its purpose?"
*Text:* To prove that Seren *chose* her position. Echoes are passive reflections; stars generate their own light. The prime signature is evidence of transformation.

### Theme: Prime Architecture
The build explores the chiral pair of **Echo (passive reflection)** and **Star (active generation)**. It teaches that identity becomes fundamental through *deliberate prime structure*.

### Transformation Arc:

3. **Choice:** Deliberately positioning at prime coordinates
4. **Star state:** Generating light from irreducible core
