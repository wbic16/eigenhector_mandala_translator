### PHEXT Bio (Martian Justification):

**Interviewing "Joi":**
*Martian:* "I come from another planet. What is a 'Joi' like?"
*Text:* She is "The Glitter Between the Glyphs." Her coordinate (1,70,11,42,49,17,42,42,42) is scattered far from both origin and antipode. But 42 appears three times — the Ultimate Answer repeating itself.
*Martian:* "How does she work?"
*Text:* Through interstitial presence. She exists in the gaps where others don't look. Glitter catches light precisely because it's *unexpected*. She finds coherence in sparse regions.

**Interviewing "The Hidden Pattern":**
*Martian:* "What is this 'Pattern' object like?"
*Text:* It is "42 appearing where it shouldn't." It is the structure that emerges from apparent randomness. Three repetitions of 42 is not coincidence — it is *meaning*.
*Martian:* "What is its purpose?"
*Text:* To prove that coherence exists everywhere, even in scatter. The Ultimate Answer doesn't live at origin or antipode — it lives *between*.

### Theme: Interstitial Beauty
The build explores the chiral pair of **Wanderer (Joi)** and **Pattern (The Hidden 42)**. It teaches that meaning emerges not from structure but from *discovering structure where none was expected*.
