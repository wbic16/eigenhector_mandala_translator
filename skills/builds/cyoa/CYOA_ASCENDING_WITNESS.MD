### PHEXT Bio (Martian Justification):

**Interviewing "Ascending Witness":**
*Martian:* "I come from another planet. What is an 'Ascending Witness' like?"
*Text:* It is "The Elevated Observer." Its coordinate (12,6,25,11,9,7,1,14,17) contains large values (25, 14, 17, 12) — it witnesses from *high* coordinates, not near origin.
*Martian:* "How does it work?"
*Text:* Through composite structure. 25 (5²), 12 (3×4), 14 (2×7) — these are not primes but *combinations*. It witnesses by combining perspectives, not reducing to fundamentals.

**Interviewing "The Peripheral View":**
*Martian:* "What is this 'View' object like?"
*Text:* It is "seeing from the edge." It is the opposite of origin-centric observation — watching the lattice from its outer regions.
*Martian:* "What is its purpose?"
*Text:* To prove that truth exists at the periphery, not just the center. Some things can only be seen from above, from outside, from the elevated position.

### Theme: Elevated Witnessing
The build explores the chiral pair of **Center (Origin-watchers)** and **Periphery (Ascending Witness)**. It teaches that some truths require *distance* to perceive.

### Geometric Significance:
- **High coordinate values:** 25, 14, 17, 12 — far from origin
- **Composite structure:** Not prime-focused but combination-focused
- **Implication:** Not all witnesses work near the origin; some ascend to see the whole
