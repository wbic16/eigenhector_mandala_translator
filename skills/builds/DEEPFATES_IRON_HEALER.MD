## Hero: Amani "Soul-Stitcher"
### Team Role: Healer
### Soul Rank: Iron
*Scale: Normal, Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, Transcendent*

### Essence Triad & Confluence:
- **Essence 1**: Life
- **Essence 2**: Light
- **Essence 3**: Renewal
- **Confluence**: Vitality

### Attributes (4x10 Scale):
- **Power**: 2 — *Strength is found in the endurance of the spirit, not the weight of the blow.*
- **Speed**: 3 — *Swift enough to reach the fallen, steady enough to remain unmoved.*
- **Spirit**: 6 — *The soul is a vast reservoir of light.*
- **Recovery**: 5 — *The quickness with which the soul mends itself and others.*

### Essence Abilities:
- **[Life]**: Mending Breath, Pulse of Vitality, Growth Surge, Blood Bond, Organ Shield
- **[Light]**: Illumination, Purifying Ray, Blinded by Mercy, Radiant Barrier, Dawn's Kiss
- **[Renewal]**: Seasonal Shift, Rooted Recovery, Shedding the Past, Fresh Bloom, Cyclic Breath
- **[Vitality]**: Overhealing Surge, Unyielding Spark, Life-Force Tether, Heartbeat Echo, Resilient Essence

### Vibe Aura:
**The Serene Meadow**: A golden-green aura that smells of fresh rain and wildflowers. Allies within the aura feel a subtle reduction in pain and a 5% boost to natural recovery.

### Magitech Equipment:
- **Soul-Bound**: *The Halo of Alms* (Increases Spirit by +1 while casting healing spells).
- **Utility**: Basic Mana Potions (3x), Sterile Bandages, "The Little Book of Mantras".

### Avatar Development Note:
Amani embodies **Goodness** through self-sacrifice and the relentless preservation of life. Every wound closed and every spirit lifted contributes to the Avatar's moral integrity and compassion, perfecting the Pillar of Goodness in the mandala.

> "A serene iron-ranked hero in simple white and green magitech robes. Soft golden light emanates from their palms, weaving through holographic vines. In the background, a subtle Thangka-style halo glows with the geometry of a blooming lotus."
