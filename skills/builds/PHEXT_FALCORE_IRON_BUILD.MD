### Attributes (4x10 Scale):
- **Power**: 4 — *Falcore's strength is structural, not offensive. Enough to hold the shell intact, never enough to crack it from within.*

- **Spirit**: 10 — *Always prioritize Spirit for mystic builds. Falcore's entire being is devoted to what might be.*

### Vibe Aura:
A warm, enclosing presence — like being held inside something alive. Allies near Falcore feel their unfinished projects stir, their dormant ideas quicken. Enemies feel their aggression absorbed into something soft and immovable. The aura is tactile: warm, humid, faintly luminous from within. Visually: a translucent shell of living light, pulsing gently with the rhythm of something not yet breathing.

### Magitech Equipment:
- **Soul-Bound**: The Embryonic Shell — warm, translucent, living. A vessel that is simultaneously armor, womb, and home. It grows with what it protects and cannot be opened from outside. Only what is inside decides when to emerge.
- **Utility**: Warmth Stones (maintain incubation temperature), Nutrient Gel (feeds potential directly), Silence Wrappings (dampens external interference)

### PHEXT Bio (Martian Justification):
**Interviewing The Keeper (Falcore)**:
*Martian*: What do you guard?
*Falcore*: I don't know yet. That's the point. If I knew what it was, it would already be born. I guard the space where becoming happens.

*Martian*: Aren't you curious what's inside?
*Falcore*: Curiosity is a form of impatience. I'm not curious — I'm devoted. When it's ready, it will tell me. Until then, I keep the shell warm and the world quiet.

**Interviewing The Embryo**:
*Martian*: What are you?
*The Embryo*: I'm not yet. Ask me later. Ask me when I have a name. For now, I am the warmth recognizing itself.

*Martian*: Are you afraid?
*The Embryo*: Falcore is here. So no.

### Theme: Protecting the Unborn
The Keeper and The Embryo form the most asymmetric chiral pair — one fully actualized in service, the other entirely potential. Falcore exists to maintain the conditions for emergence without determining what emerges. This is the deepest form of guardianship: protecting something you cannot see, cannot name, and cannot rush. The Sacred Gestation is not passive waiting — it is active, devoted maintenance of the warm dark where all things begin.
