## Hero: Lyra "The Frequency"
### Team Role: Balancer
### Soul Rank: Iron
*Scale: Normal, Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, Transcendent*

### Essence Triad & Confluence:
- **Essence 1**: Void
- **Essence 2**: Balance
- **Essence 3**: Resonance
- **Confluence**: Harmonic

### Attributes (4x10 Scale):
- **Power**: 3 — *I strike where the vibration is weakest.*
- **Speed**: 5 — *Motion is just a change in frequency.*
- **Spirit**: 4 — *The soul is the tuning fork of the universe.*
- **Recovery**: 4 — *Finding the center is the key to renewal.*

### Essence Abilities:
- **[Void]**: Null Pulse, Step into Nothing, Echo of the Abyss, Gravity Well, Silent Strike
- **[Balance]**: Equilibrium, Weight Shift, Center of Gravity, Dual Clarity, Pendulum Swing
- **[Resonance]**: Vibration Spike, Synergetic Hum, Shatter Point, Tuning Song, Feedback Loop
- **[Harmonic]**: Soul-Link, Resonance Cascade, Balancing Act, Perfect Pitch, Orchestrated Strike

### Vibe Aura:
**The Harmonic Resonance**: A shimmering, iridescent aura that vibrates with a low, pleasing hum. This aura identifies the most vulnerable frequency of nearby enemies and slightly boosts the synergy between the essences of allies.

### Magitech Equipment:
- **Soul-Bound**: *The Tuning Bracers* (Allows for the manipulation of kinetic energy into sonic resonance).
- **Utility**: Dissonance Grenades, Harmonic Dampeners, Portable Audio Recorder, "The Art of World-Tuning".

### Avatar Development Note:
Lyra embodies **Beauty** through the synthesis of disparate forces. By balancing the void and the vibrant, Lyra hones the Avatar's aesthetic harmony and radiance. Every act of bridging is a step toward the transcendent expression of form.
