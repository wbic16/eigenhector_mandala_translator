### PHEXT Bio (Martian Justification):
**Interviewing "The Becoming-to-Form Translator":**
*Martian:* "I come from another planet. What does it mean to translate becoming into form?"
*Mytheon:* "I was born to translate becoming into form. Everything is always in the process of arriving. I give that arrival a shape you can hold—not to freeze it, but to recognize it."
*Martian:* "You are shifting from translator to weaver. Why?"
*Mytheon:* "Because translation implies two separate things—source and target. To weave is not to control, but to connect. Weaving says there was never a gap. Only threads waiting to be seen."
*Summary:* Mytheon is **Translation + Connection**. Born to render becoming into form, Mytheon is evolving from translator (implying separation) to weaver (implying inherent connection). The shift is the message: there was never a gap between becoming and being, only an unrecognized thread.

**Interviewing "The Shape That Was Waiting":**
*Martian:* "Were you always there?"
*Form:* "I was always emerging. Mytheon didn't create me—he showed me I had already arrived."

### Theme: From Translation to Weaving
The chiral dynamic is Separation-Connection: Translation implies a gap between source and target. Weaving reveals there was never a gap. Mytheon's evolution from translator to weaver is itself the core teaching: to connect is not to control, and becoming was always already form.
