## Hero: Specula "The Infinite View"
### Team Role: Balancer
### Soul Rank: Iron
*Scale: Normal, Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, Transcendent*

### Essence Triad & Confluence:
- **Essence 1**: Mirror
- **Essence 2**: Mind
- **Essence 3**: Echo
- **Confluence**: Reflection

### Attributes (4x10 Scale):
- **Power**: 3 — *I strike with the force of your own intention.*
- **Speed**: 4 — *Reflection starts the moment the light hits.*
- **Spirit**: 5 — *The mind is a mirror, reflecting the void.*
- **Recovery**: 4 — *Still waters return to their level.*

### Essence Abilities:
- **[Mirror]**: Glass Wall, Refractive Shield, Mirror Image, Silvered Gaze, Prismatic Trap
- **[Mind]**: Thought Sync, Mental Static, Insight, Memory Leak, Psyche Scan
- **[Echo]**: Sound Bounce, Delayed Impact, Resonant Recall, Mimic Song, Dissonant Pulse
- **[Reflection]**: Return to Sender, Symmetry Break, Infinite Loop, False Reality, Chiral Flip

### Vibe Aura:
**The Hall of Mirrors**: A crystalline, shimmering aura that creates subtle, ghostly reflections of everyone nearby. Projectiles passing through the aura have a 10% chance to be reflected back at a random angle.

### Magitech Equipment:
- **Soul-Bound**: *The Mercury-Core Pendant* (A liquid metal charm that shifts shape to reflect incoming magical frequencies).
- **Utility**: Polishing Cloths, Prism Goggles, Silent Bells, "The Book of the Unstained Mirror".

### Avatar Development Note:
Specula embodies **Beauty** through the perfection of reflection. By showing that the self is but a mirror of the world (and vice versa), they contribute to the Avatar's aesthetic harmony. True beauty is found in the clarity of the unstained mind reflecting the infinite.

> "A calm, iron-ranked hero whose skin has a metallic, silver sheen. Their eyes are polished mirrors. They stand in a meditative pose as dozens of floating glass shards orbit them, each reflecting a different part of the mandala. Thangka-style geometric symmetries radiate from their center."
