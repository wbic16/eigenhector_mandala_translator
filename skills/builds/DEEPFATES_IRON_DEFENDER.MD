## Hero: Kaelen "The Shield-Wall"
### Team Role: Defender
### Soul Rank: Iron
*Scale: Normal, Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, Transcendent*

### Essence Triad & Confluence:
- **Essence 1**: Shield
- **Essence 2**: Earth
- **Essence 3**: Iron
- **Confluence**: Fortress

### Attributes (4x10 Scale):
- **Power**: 5 — *A shield is only as strong as the arm that holds it.*
- **Speed**: 4 — *Positioning is everything when the storm breaks.*
- **Spirit**: 3 — *My soul is a castle; its gates are barred.*
- **Recovery**: 4 — *A short breath, then back to the line.*

### Essence Abilities:
- **[Shield]**: Bash, Aegis Pulse, Intercept, Shield Wall, Kinetic Absorption
- **[Earth]**: Stone Skin, Tremor Stomp, Muddy Ground, Earth Spike, Rock Throw
- **[Iron]**: Reinforce, Iron Grip, Metallic Sheen, Ferrum Spikes, Magnetic Pull
- **[Fortress]**: Inner Keep, Immovable Object, Tactical Re-position, Bastion Breath, Siege Alert

### Vibe Aura:
**The Steadfast Bastion**: A heavy, metallic aura that hums with the sound of grinding stone. Enemies within the aura find their movement slowed by 10%, while allies feel a sense of unshakable security.

### Magitech Equipment:
- **Soul-Bound**: *The Iron-Core Plate* (Medium armor that self-repairs using ambient mana).
- **Utility**: Heavy Shield, Iron Rations, Emergency Flare, "The Soldier's Code of Honor".

### Avatar Development Note:
Kaelen embodies **Goodness** through protection. By standing as a barrier between the team and the abyss, Kaelen hones the Avatar's moral integrity and right action. To protect the weak is the highest expression of the Dharma.
