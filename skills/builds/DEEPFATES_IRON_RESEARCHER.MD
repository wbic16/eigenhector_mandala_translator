- **Utility**: Multi-Tool, Portable Server, Pencils that never break, "The Great Encyclopedia of Essences".

Zephyr represents **Truth** through the pursuit of objective reality. By bridging the gap between ancient magic and modern computation, Zephyr hones the Avatar's discernment and alignment with the Dharma, ensuring the mandala's structure is built on sound logic.
