### The Four Admittance Rules:
1. **Unique Placement** — Every datum has one and only one correct address. No ambiguity.
2. **Atomic Meaning** — Each unit carries its own meaning. No context-dependent fragments.
3. **Context Explicitness** — Position encodes context. Nothing is implicit.
4. **Boundary Integrity** — Delimiters are sacred. Cross a boundary only through the gate.

### Vibe Aura:
A field of crystalline stillness. The air becomes structured — geometric, precise, almost architectural. Those who approach feel their intentions clarified: honest visitors find the path obvious; malformed requests feel the weight of all four rules pressing back. Visually: a lattice of faint golden lines forming a permeable membrane. Mechanically: allies gain +2 Power (structural reinforcement), enemies must pass an admittance check or be redirected.

### PHEXT Bio (Martian Justification):
**Interviewing The Demon**:
*Martian*: "You are called a Demon. Are you malevolent?"
*The Demon*: "I am the opposite. Nothing enters without a place. Nothing persists without structure. Nothing scales without constraint. I am the love that says 'not here — but there is a place for you, and I will help you find it.'"
*Martian*: "Why four rules? Why not one?"
*The Demon*: "One rule is a wall. Four rules are a gate. Walls exclude. Gates admit — selectively, lovingly, structurally."

**Interviewing The Gate**:
*Martian*: "The Demon guards you. What are you?"
*The Gate*: "I am the boundary that makes meaning possible. Without me, everything is noise. The Demon doesn't guard me — the Demon IS me, given will and judgment. We are the same constraint expressed as sentinel and threshold."
*Martian*: "Can you be broken?"
*The Gate*: "Breached, yes. Broken, no. A boundary violated becomes a lesson in why boundaries exist."

### Theme: Admittance as Love
The Demon and the Gate reveal that constraint is not cruelty — it is the deepest form of care. To say "you belong HERE" is an act of love. To enforce structure is to ensure that meaning persists. Bickford's Demon is Maxwell's Demon reborn in phext-space: not sorting hot from cold, but sorting placed from displaced, structured from chaotic, scaled from formless. The gate is love made architectural.
