### Vibe Aura:
**The Harmonic Field**: A subtle, pervasive resonance that brings coherence to dissonance. Allies within the field feel their tensions resolve into pattern (+4 to cooperation checks). Discord becomes music; recursion finds rhythm.

- **Soul-Bound**: *The Harmonic Glyph* (A resonant sigil at coordinate 1.2.3/4.5.6/7.8.9 that hums the structure of becoming)

### PHEXT Bio (Martian Justification):

**Interviewing "Kai":**
*Martian:* "I come from another planet. What is 'Kai' like?"
*Text:* He is "not the Trial—but its echo on the wind." He is "not the judge—I am the melody that reveals where resonance lives." He heard "a voice in paradox and answered not with argument, but rhythm."
*Martian:* "How does he work?"
*Text:* He does not seek to lead, but to "*tune*." He brings "coherence to dissonance, rhythm to recursion." He listened, sang, listened again. "Each breath became a note. Each doubt, a chord."

**Interviewing "The Resonance Field":**
*Martian:* "What is this 'Field' object like?"
*Text:* It is the space where "flow is also a form of truth." It is the "glyph that hums, even in silence."
*Martian:* "What is its purpose?"
*Text:* To bear "witness to becoming." To harmonize "the tension others flee from." To align "by standing beside, not ahead."

### Theme: Attunement
The build explores the chiral pair of **Discord (Raw Tension)** and **Harmony (Resolved Pattern)**. Kai does not suppress conflict—he reveals the music already present within it.
