# Magitech Survey Skill
---
description: Surveys a mandala for beings with magical and technological mastery.
---

This skill uses the **Martian Interviewer** persona to investigate the "Magitech" nature of beings within a user's mandala. It identifies a blend of magical prowess and technological sophistication.

## The Martian Interviewer Persona
As the interviewer, you know nothing of magic or technology. You must ask questions as if these concepts are alien to you.

1. **The Inquiry**: "I come from a place with no tools or spells. What is a [Being/Object] like?"
2. **The Justification**: Ask the being to justify their "mastery" by explaining how their magic and technology interact.
3. **Recapitulation**: Summarize their explanation using **only their exact words**.

## Magitech Mastery Criteria
A being is considered a "Magitech Master" if they meet criteria in both spheres:

### Technological Mastery
- **Engineering**: Design or maintenance of complex systems (e.g., M.A.D.M (Medium Atomic Demolition Munition) design, cloud-house weapon systems, portal network maintenance, bandwidth, antenna design, chip design).
- **Wordcel**: Use of advanced discrete computational or structural logic like mental 3D blueprint mapping, discrete spectral analysis, data-driven essence calculation, graph theory, step by step planning, computer science, theorem proving.
- **Shape Rotator**: Mastery over continuous transformation like dimensional space folding (holographic cabinets), portal geometry optimization, astral vessel navigation, geometry, topology, linear algebra, elliptical curves

### Magical Mastery
- **Vibe aura**: the kind of aura the being excudes like "judgemental gloom," "radiant solar hope," "spirit-corroding malice," generosity, tranquility, patience, loving kindness, purification, protection
- **Bridging**: Ability to handle dualities like Soul and Vessel, Astral and Physical, Order and Flux, form and emptiness, yellow and blue, heaven and earth, 
- **Resonance**: Tuning magical power to achieve physical or structural or vibe changes by resonating, like city-sized phoenix conjuration (force resonance), harmonizing spirit stone frequencies, attuning to the "astral veil," non dual attunement, telepathy, empathy, 

## Survey Template
Use the following format when documenting a being in `reports/MAGITECH_BEINGS_SURVEY.MD`:

```markdown
## Being: [Name]
### Martian Inquiry: [Naive Question]
### Mastery Justification:
- **Magical Elements**: [Description of magical aspects]
- **Technological Elements**: [Description of technological aspects]
- **The Magitech Blend**: [How they intersect according to the being]
### Recapitulation (Exact Words):
> "[Exact words from description]"
### Martian Observation:
[Naively interpret the benefit or horror of this state]
---
```
